The Lord of the Rings:
The Fellowship of the Ring:
Trick-Taking Game:
The Road Goes Ever On
Randomizer

Randomly select one Gold Numbered card to flip to burdened side.

Victory: Long (finish all characters)

  • Frodo must be always chosen, and always leads the first trick. (Ignore instructions to do otherwise.)
  • Black Rider card must be used in one round. On chosen round, the card is given to a random non-burdened character.
    • (!) If the Black Rider is the Unseen, it must be given to a character that uses a Threat card.
  • Players choose when and who to give chosen Gift to, unless it specifies who gets it.
  • All interaction with the lost card are optional. If multiple characters interact with the lost card, the players decide which of the characters takes the card.
